Work Distribution

For this project to have been possible, the overall goal was split into tasks that was distributed to each team member to work out. Here is how our work was split:

Data Preprocessing

The data preprocessing team worked on splitting our data into the different food groups such as "dairy free" or "vegetarian". They helped clean out our data that we used for model training. This job was completed by none other than Tony and Yatin!

Expansion

Lincoln and Alex worked as a part of the expansion team. They contributed to image scraping on Google to collect correct food images that corresponded to the recipes. They also made a difficulty rating system to make it even easier to choose a recipe.

Aitextgen

The Aitextgen team consisting of Sherry and Chloe experimented with ways to train models and figured out how word generation works. They worked on how to use a gpt_2 model while the others got the data ready for training.

Front End

The front end development team worked on this very website! They wrote all the text you see here and even added custom images. This team was made up of Lincoln, Alex, Sherry, Yatin, Tony and Chloe. (Basically everyone)

Back End

Tony and Yatin worked on back end development of our website. Not only did they train all the models, but they also helped connect our back end text generations to the front end website.

Instructor

Special thanks to our instructor Jason for making this project possible. From providing us with resources on coding to helping us debug, we really could not have done it without him!
