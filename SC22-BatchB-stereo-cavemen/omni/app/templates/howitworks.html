How does Andrew work?

with GSAP ScrollTrigger & Locomotive Scroll

Our model Andrew uses our filtered data of every Yu-Gi-Oh card to generate a completely random card which can be used in a real Yu-Gi-Oh match!

To get our data, we filtered out all cards with the “Pendulum Effect” which complicated the card battle, filtered out all cards which were not monsters, organized the monster cards based on their attribute, and formatted the output to be ~desc~*ATK; DEF; LEVEL; RACE; to present the monsters attributes such as health, atack, race, ect.

Lastly, we got the output data and transferred the data onto a template of a Yu-Gi-Oh card, thus ending the process and ending with a unique never before seen Yu-Gi-Oh card!
